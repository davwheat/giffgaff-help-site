FAQs

What will I need to activate my SIM?

To activate your SIM, you’ll need a credit/debit card, or a top-up voucher.

Once you’ve activated your SIM pop it into an unlocked phone and you’re ready to go.

How do I activate with a voucher?

Pop in your activation code and follow the quick, easy steps to activate your SIM. On the payment page, select voucher and enter your 16 digit voucher code.

Can I keep my old phone number?

Sure thing. We’d love to bring your old number to giffgaff. After you’ve activated your SIM, we’ll give you a link to transfer your old number. You’ll just need your PAC code from your previous network.

Can I do a SIM swap?

If you're already a member, a SIM swap will let you use a different SIM if yours is the wrong size, missing or faulty. Enter the code from your new SIM and click 'Activate your SIM'. On the next screen, log in with your giffgaff member name or phone number.

Can’t find your activation code?

No worries. You can enter your 13 digit SIM serial number, which is located on the back of your SIM card, before 4Ggg4. In some cases, this may be 16 or 19 digits.
